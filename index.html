Forged Motus, LLC

80V BLDC FOC Platform

As CEO and Hardware Lead, led a small team to build a field‑oriented control brushless motor driver. It includes an 18‑bit encoder, CAN‑FD, and runs at 80 volts and 45 amps.

Main Projects

Guide Dog, Brushless Motor Driver and Reinforcement Learning

Built a 24 volt brushless motor driver that is a drop‑in replacement for the Go2 drivers on Unitree robot dogs for use in a robot dog. Also took on the challenge of using Isaac Sim and Isaac Lab for reinforcement learning.

Dash Brushless Motor Driver and Electrical Stack

Building a high‑power 48‑volt, 70‑amp brushless driver for a new humanoid robot and designing various electrical parts. Currently working a Ethernet to CAN-FD build using altium.

Prestoe: Upgrading a Humanoid Robot

Upgrading Prestoe to 80 volts using new battery and motor driver, while making him faster using CAN-FD.

UMass CubeSat Lab: 6U CubeSat BMS and Power System

Designed a custom 6U power system—from battery chemistry to PCB design and solar panel manufacturing.

UMass CubeSat Lab: Space-Ready PCIe to USB 3.1

Implementing a USB 3.1 interface using the existing M.2 slot on nanosatellite flight computers to enable compatibility with hyperspectral cameras.

Automated Warehouse Delivery Rover

Used Nav2, SLAM Toolbox, Intel RealSense cameras, and ROS 2 to build a safe, reliable system for transporting chemicals from the shipping room to laboratories.

Team Projects

UMass Robotics: RoboDog

Serving as Electrical Lead and Project Manager to build a custom quadruped for future research.

UMass Robotics: SAM Humanoid Torso

Served as Electrical Lead and Project Manager; developed a custom SPI‑to‑CAN board and designed the humanoid electrical stack for VR control.

UMass Rocket Team: In‑Flight Deployable Drone

Built and deployed a drone located near the nose cone of a 14‑foot rocket; it deployed during descent using onboard sensors and GPS to perform an autonomous landing.

6U Orientation Demo with 3‑DOF Gimbal

Team project to prove and test orientation control for a 6U CubeSat using a 3‑DOF gimbal. Demonstrated closed‑loop attitude control concepts and validated sensor‑fusion and actuation on a benchtop rig.
