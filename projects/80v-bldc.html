80V BLDC FOC Platform

FOC · Motor Control

As CEO and Hardware Lead of Forged Motus, LLC, I led a small team to design and build a high-performance field-oriented control (FOC) brushless motor driver from the ground up.

The platform runs at 80 volts and 45 amps continuous, making it suitable for high-torque robotics applications. It features an 18-bit absolute encoder for precision position sensing, CAN-FD for high-speed communication, and a full FOC implementation for efficient torque control at any speed.

Every aspect of the design — schematic, PCB layout, firmware, and enclosure — was handled by the team. The goal was to produce a driver that competes with commercial offerings at a fraction of the cost, purpose-built for research and advanced robotics platforms.
